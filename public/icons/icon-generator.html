How to Install TaskCheck as a Web App:

  1. On iPhone/iPad:
    • Open Safari and go to your TaskCheck website
    • Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
    • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
    • Tap "Add" to confirm
  2. On Android:
    • Open Chrome and go to your TaskCheck website
    • Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right
    • Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
    • Tap "Add" or "Install" to confirm
  3. On Desktop (Chrome/Edge):
    • Look for the install icon in the address bar
    • Click it and select "Install"
    • The app will be added to your desktop and start menu
